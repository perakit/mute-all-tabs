Privacy Policy for "Mute All Tabs" Extension

Effective Date: August 2, 2025

Data Collection

The "Mute All Tabs" extension does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal or identifiable information from users. It only interacts with browser tabs for the sole purpose of muting audio.

Permissions

The extension requires access to tab data (tabs) solely to identify and mute open tabs. No tab content, history, or personal browsing information is accessed or collected.

User Privacy

Your privacy is fully respected. The extension operates entirely within your browser and does not communicate with any external servers or services.
